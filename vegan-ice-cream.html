I DREAM OF ICE CREAM

In 2015 I had a dream to start a vegan ice cream line. Four years plus two babies later, it’s finally a reality.

Starting a food product is one of the most challenging things I’ve ever done. Imagine what you think goes into the process then times it by a hundred, or more. But luckily for me, I’m always up for a challenge and thrive on getting sh*t done.

I’m so proud of our vegan, refined sugar free, gluten free ice cream, that was created to bring comfort just like the ‘real deal’. It is now is full swing, small batch production mode at our commercial kitchen located in Karratha, Western Australia.
